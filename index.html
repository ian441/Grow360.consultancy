Assessment 05

Enhanced Internal Control Assessment

The Enhanced Internal Control Assessment Tool evaluates an organization's internal control environment across the five COSO Internal Control – Integrated Framework (2013) components.

View full assessment

It examines governance and culture, risk identification, control operating effectiveness, information systems, and ongoing monitoring. It is aligned with global benchmarks and UAE regulatory requirements, including Federal Law 11/1992, Law 7/2015, DFSA Standards, and ISO 31000.

  • 01
    Control Environment & Governance
    Ethics, board oversight, structure, and competence of control personnel
  • 02
    Risk Assessment & Identification
    Risk identification, fraud risk, compliance risk, and change management
  • 03
    Control Activities & Operating Effectiveness
    Authorization, segregation of duties, and physical and access controls
  • 04
    Information & Communication Systems
    Quality, flow, and use of information supporting internal control
  • 05
    Monitoring Activities & Evaluation
    Ongoing and separate evaluations of control effectiveness over time
